


🌟 Project Overview

Campus-Crafts is a hyper-local handmade marketplace designed specifically for college student creators.
The platform combines:

🛍️ Handmade product selling
📱 Social-media-style discovery
🎥 Behind-the-scenes creator content
📍 Hyper-local campus commerce
🤝 Community-driven shopping experiences

Student creators can monetize hobbies such as:

Crochet
Resin art
Clay art
Anime merchandise
Paintings
Custom gifts
Stickers
Handmade accessories

Buyers can discover unique aesthetic products from creators within their campus or neighborhood.

❗ Problem Statement

Most traditional eCommerce platforms:

Feel too corporate and generic
Lack visibility for small student creators
Do not support hyper-local campus-based selling
Ignore Gen-Z social shopping behavior
Make handmade products feel less personal

Student creators struggle to:

Build an audience
Gain trust
Sell locally
Showcase their creative process

💡 Solution & Unique Selling Point (USP)

Craftora creates a social-first marketplace focused on:

🎨 Creator identity
📸 Aesthetic-driven discovery
📍 Hyper-local selling
💬 Community interaction
🎥 BTS (Behind-the-scenes) storytelling
